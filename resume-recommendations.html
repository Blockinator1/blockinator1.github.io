Recommendations

Silas Fay is an enthusiastic student with a passion for learning. I have known Silas since the beginning of the 2022-23 school year as his math teacher. He took Secondary Math 3 Honors his sophomore year and University of Utah Calculus his junior year, and he has done excellent work in both classes. He has earned an A on every exam due to his natural curiosity about math and the way new math topics are related to what he has already learned. If he notices a relationship between the new topic and a previous topic, he will fully investigate it on his own by experimenting with Desmos (a computer program that visualizes mathematics) and discovering aspects of the topic that go even beyond what is expected at the level of the class. He enjoys talking about math with other students, and he has a unique perspective and creative ideas that allow other students to expand their understanding as well.
